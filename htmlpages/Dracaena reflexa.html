plant Dracaena reflexa

The plant of our study is Jamaica, whose scientific name is Dracaena reflexa "Song of Jamaica",
From tropical plants native to Madagascar, it is one of the distinctive plants growing as a small-sized shrub with its shiny,
green-tinted leaves with creamy veins of color in the middle, Maturity is 3-5 metres high, while prevalence is 1-1.5 metres.
This makes it a suitable option for filling spaces in homes and work environments to bring beauty and style to the spaces in which they are placed.
